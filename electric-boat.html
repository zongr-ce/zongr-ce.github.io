Electric Boat – Battery & Propulsion System

Overview

Designed and built a dual-battery electric propulsion system for a competition boat, prioritizing safety, efficiency, and reliability.

My Role

Power Systems Engineer responsible for battery design, BMS integration, testing, and propulsion validation.

Technical Highlights

  • Two custom 6s8p 18650 Li-ion battery packs (24 Ah)
  • JK-BMS integration for cell balancing and protection
  • Spot welding and vibration isolation
  • Dual 1 kW Blue Robotics T500 thrusters
  • Differential thrust steering (no mechanical rudder)

Results

  • ~2.5 hours runtime at 8 knots
  • ~10 A average current draw per motor at full throttle
  • Handled ~50 A load testing with minimal temperature rise
  • 2nd Place – PEP 2024 Displacement Hull Division
