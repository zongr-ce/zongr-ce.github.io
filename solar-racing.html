Badger Solar Racing – PDC Current Sense

Overview

Designed and implemented a current-sensing solution for the power distribution center (PDC) of a solar racing vehicle, supporting safer power monitoring and system-level visibility.

What I Did

  • Developed a PDC current-sensing approach for monitoring power usage.
  • Supported communication between modules using CAN (Controller Area Network).
  • Designed PCB hardware (control-board related) in Altium Designer.
  • Collaborated with the team to align design decisions with integration constraints.

System Images

Results

  • Delivered a current-sensing solution concept for the PDC monitoring stack.
  • Strengthened experience with CAN-based module communication and PCB design workflows.
  • Improved system-level understanding of power distribution, measurement, and integration constraints.
